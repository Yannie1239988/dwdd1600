Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, famous for his large, bold signature.
Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration of Independence.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned statesman, inventor, and diplomat.
John Adams
Strong advocate for independence and later the 2nd U.S. President.
Samuel Adams
Leader in the movement toward independence and organizer of protests like the Boston Tea Party.

These figures are among the most widely recognized, though 56 delegates in total signed the document.
