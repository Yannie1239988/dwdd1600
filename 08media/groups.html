Popular KPOP Groups

Famous Groups

Here's a list of the most popular K-pop groups as of recent years, based on global recognition, streaming numbers, social media presence, and concert tours:

BTS
Global superstars known for hits like "Dynamite" and "Butter," with a massive international fanbase and record-breaking achievements.
BLACKPINK
Worldwide sensation with hits like "DDU-DU DDU-DU" and "Kill This Love," famous for their style, stage presence, and global tours.
EXO
Legendary boy group with a huge fanbase, known for songs like "Love Shot" and "Growl."
TWICE
Popular girl group known for catchy tracks like "Fancy" and "TT," with a strong presence in Asia and beyond.
Stray Kids
Fast-rising group recognized globally for energetic performances and self-produced hits like "God’s Menu."

The K-pop industry thrives on diverse concepts, dedicated fanbases, and innovative performances, making it one of the most influential forces in modern pop music worldwide.
